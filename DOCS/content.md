# WHY MAIKR STUDIO
## Why We Created MAIKR Studio
In 2024, we noticed that LLMs were excellent at generating code, but there’s a significant gap when it comes to taking that code to production.

Even with AI integrated into modern development tools, building beyond simple applications remains extremely challenging. Even experienced users struggled to get apps working properly.

The need for expert guidance is what sparked the creation of MAIKR Studio.

# MISSION
## Our Mission
We wanted to build a place where people could come in and, with the help of AI and our experts, start building real, working applications easily.
